It was established under Section 3 of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act, 1956, and was founded in 1984 as a self-financing institution called the Vellore Engineering College. The Union Ministry of Human Resources Development conferred University status on Vellore Engineering College in 2001. VIT is headed by its founder and Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, a former Parliamentarian and Minister in the Tamil Nadu Government. In recognition of his service to India in offering world class education, he was conferred an honorary doctorate by the West Virginia University, USA. Sankar Viswanathan Vice-President and Kadhambari S. Viswanathan, is the Assistant Vice President. Dr. Anand A. Samuel is the Vice-Chancellor and Dr. P. Gunasekaran is the Pro-Vice-Chancellor.

Quick Facts

  • NAAC has re-accredited VIT with an 'A' grade.
  • A 360-acre eco-friendly campus with over 50.83 lakh sq.ft. built-up space at gollagudem.
  • A 170 acre eco-friendly second campus at Tadepalligudem.
  • VIT Tadepalligudem has 6285 students
  • Students from 50 countries as well as from every state in India
  • 381 faculty and 147 staff members are in VIT TADEPALLIGUDEM
  • VIT has been accredited by TCS, Wipro and Cognizant for academic collaboration and placements.
